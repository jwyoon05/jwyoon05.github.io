I am an undergraduate student in the [School of Biological Sciences](https://biosci.snu.ac.kr/) at Seoul National University. I am also an undergraduate intern in the [Steinegger Lab](https://steineggerlab.com/en/), advised by Prof. Martin Steinegger.  

My research interest lies in developing integrated computational pipelines applicable to predict biomolecular interactions and design molecular binders capable to be used in disease treatment. My past research experience includes improving [Folddisco](https://search.foldseek.com/folddisco), an open-source software that can search protein motifs in protein structures.

I am passionate about developing softwares and DL models applicable in researches in the field of computational biology. See my [GitHub](https://github.com/jwyoon05) for more.
